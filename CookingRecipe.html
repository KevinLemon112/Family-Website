This is going to be a cooking recipe on how my dad makes his style of pizza.

  • Ingredients:
    1. Dough
    2. Flower
    3. Mozzarella
    4. Pepperoni
    5. Yeast
    6. Tomato Sauce
    7. Artichokes
    8. Crushed Red Pepper
    9. Bacon Bits
  • Steps to Making the Pizza:
    1. Buy pre-made dough and roll it up with a roller to make a even circle.
    2. Buy pre-made tomato sauce and spread it over the dough across evenly.
    3. Add the ingredients and toppings listed above (including meat).
    4. Add the mozzarella cheese.
    5. Put the pizza in the oven and heated until the yeast builds upward and you see the pizza is turning browner.
    6. Take the pizza out of the oven and let it cool for about ten minutes.
    7. Enjoy the Pizza!
